A quick start guide to creating your first website

Are you wondering how to create your first website? Then follow the steps listed on this website to learn how to set up your own website. I will be covering everything from finding your first host to understanding cPanel. You will get your site up and running in no time!

Things to consider before starting

Your niche

What kind of website do you want to create? Do you want to make a portfolio of your artwork or perhaps a blog on a topic that interests you?

Deciding a niche is the first thing you want to think of when you are creating a website.

From there, you will be able to build up your content.

Choosing your web host

You need to think/decide what host you are going to go with. Do you want to go with a free or a paid host?

You also need to decide what services you are going with. How much bandwidth and disk space will you need. For more information about web hosts click here.

Subdomain or domain?

Do you want to spend around ten dollars a year for a domain name? Or perhaps go with a subdomain, which if free.

Example of a domain name: squarespace.com

Or a subdomain: mywebsite.squarespace.com

How much money?

How much money are you willing to pay for your website?

Building a website ranges from being completely free to paying for different services!
